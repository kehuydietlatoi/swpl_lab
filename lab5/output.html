Station Tuning

There are various ways of tuning into a station.

Automatic tuning

Press the ↑ or ↓ button.

The radio tunes into the next receiavable station

If you hold down ↑ or ↓, the station search will run forwards or backwards quickly.

Manual tuning with << or >>

Press << or >>, the frequency will change in short intervals downwards or upwards.

If you hold << or >> down, the frequency scan will accelerate.

You can also use keypad for tuning.
